Welcome to the world of Underwater Hockey! Inside this site you will find strategies to improve your game over time.

In the Equipment section you'll find everything you need to know about what you need for the game, along with standard brands that most players use. And of course some links to companies that sell the specialized items.

Game Basics will give you an introduction to the game.

Puck Skills offers puck handling moves you should practice, master and continue to use as warmups as you improve.

The Basic Workouts section will get you started with some beginner workouts to get you started using fins and working out your lungs.

Links will take you to sites all about underwater hockey and places to buy your new underwater hockey gear.

 

  

So you've learned and mastered the basics and now you want a challenge! This side of the site will get you kicking your fins a bit harder.

Inside the Advanced Strategies you'll find some more complex individual ideas, as well as team strategies to improve your level of play.

The Advanced Skills section offers tougher puck handling skills for you to master.
