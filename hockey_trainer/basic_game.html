Game Basics

  • There are 6 players on either side of the pool. One side has black sticks, the other white. Each person lines up on the wall - holding the wall with one hand.

  • The puck is placed in the middle of the pool, on the bottom. Goals are placed on the bottom of the pool along the wall that the players are lined up on.

  • In official play, there is a gong struck once to signify the start of play; In practice, someone on one side of the pool yells "sticks up" and someone on the either side yells "go."

  • The typical formation of players is 3 forward (offensive) players and 3 back (defensive) players.

  • In order to score a goal, the puck must be pushed with the stick into the opponent's goal.

  • The team with the most goals at the end of 2 halves (each 12 minutes long) is the winner.
