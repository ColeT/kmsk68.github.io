Where to Buy Gear

  

Other Useful UWH sites

CanAm Underwater Hockey Gear
A site where you will find all of your hockey needs. Carries a large selection of different stick designs. Located in the US.

True Blue
Another good site for underwater gear. Located in Australia.

Leisure Pro
Carries Mares HP fins, Sphera mask, and snorkels. Sometimes has good deals and free shipping.

Amazon
Also occasionally carries fins, masks, and snorkels at decent prices.

  

Underwater Society of America
Has rules along with other helpful information.

USA UWH
A site specifically for underwater hockey. Provides more information on reffing, more workouts and a calendar of hockey tournaments.

Underwater Hockey Tourist
Has information on practices and contacts for clubs around the world.

DC Beltway Bottomfeeders
Website for the DC underwater hockey team.
