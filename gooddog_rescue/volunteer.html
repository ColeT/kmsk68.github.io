Volunteer

Want to help out?  Have a special skill? 


Good Dog! Rescue can always use help in any number of areas:  

  • Fundraising,
  • bookkeeping,
  • transportation,
  • event coordination. 

A great way to start out is by helping at our adoption events.  You'll spend a Saturday afternoon holding a dog for potential adopters to meet their potential new family member.   For your time you will be repaid with love and affection from your furry charge. We also strongly encourage giving lots of belly rubs, hugs and kisses in return.  Our adoption events happen twice a month, so there are plenty of opportunities to help out.  Come as often as you like! 

If you think you would like to volunteer and help,
please contact us at:
info@gooddogrescueva.org
or come and visit us at one of our monthly adoption events
