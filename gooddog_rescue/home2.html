Welcome

 

Good Dog! Rescue is a small but dedicated group of volunteers. We rescue dogs who are at risk and homeless through no fault of their own. Once in our care, these companion animals are fully vaccinated and spayed or neutered. We also provide for those animals who need additional time and medical care before going to a new home. We are an all-breed rescue, but with a special place in our hearts and in our rescue for the "misunderstood" breeds,especially Pitbulls and American Staffordshires.

 

We are always in need of more foster homes. If you think you might be able to offer a foster home please visit the foster section of our web site and please contact us at info@gooddogrescueva.org or (703) 626-7410
